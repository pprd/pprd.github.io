The 6 classes

Symbol ASCII Short description
A [A] Uses tracking cookies to display advertising
B [B] Stores credit card or payment information without opt-in
C [C] Collects and stores name and contact information (firstname, lastname, address, email, etc.)
D [D] Collects and stores gender or ethnicity information
E [E] Collects and stores geolocalisation data (IP, city, etc. including hardware and software data)
? [?] No class yet
! [!] Currently under review

The criteria

Icon ASCII Short description
(#) Stores credit card or payment information without opt-in
(i) Collects and stores name and contact information (firstname, lastname, address, email, etc.)
(Me) Collects and stores gender, ethnicity or religion information
(IP) Collects and stores geolocalisation data (IP, city, etc. including hardware and software data)
(H) Uses cookies to collect and store non-anonymously browsing history / advertising profile
(->) Shares part or totality of collected data with other websites
(Del) Does not allow to completely delete your account and associated data
